About us
1/3 of all the food we buy we throw away Service organizations want access to excess food, but there are barriers — locating the excess food, establishing their credentials, and efficiently deploying resources to transport food. Akshay Patr breaks down these barriers and empowers groups with perishable resources to donate to charities serving the needy.
